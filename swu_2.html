SWU 2 - Southwest University (Chen, Chen)¶

Processing of Emotional Words under the High or Low Perceptual Load

Sample Description¶

This sample is comprised of 27 subjects. Each subject underwent anatomical scans and two resting state fMRI scans as part of an experiment on emotional processing.
