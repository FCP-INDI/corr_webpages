Sample Description¶

Typically developing participants were scanned at least 2 years apart. Resting fMRI was collected with eyes open with the instructions: “Keep your eyes open and remain awake. Let thoughts pass through your mind without focusing on any particular mental activity.” A blank screen was present for visual stimulation.
