Sample Description
------------------
Forty-nine neurotypical adults underwent 2 resting scans less than an hour apart as part of a larger study protocol. *[Confirm eyes open, get info about stimuli/instructions from Mike]*. Subjects also completed two 64-direction DTI scans, as well as IQ testing with using the Weschler Abbreviated Scale of Intelligence.
