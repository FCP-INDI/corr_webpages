Sample Description
------------------
This sample includes 30 healthy adults; Each participant received ten scans across one month, one scan every three days. Five modalities (EPI/ASL/T1/DTI/T2) of brain images were acquired for all subjects. During functional scanning, subjects were presented with a fixation cross and were instructed to keep their eyes open, relax and move as little as possible while observing the fixation cross. Subjects were also instructed not to engage in breath counting or meditation.
