Data Description
================

CoRR includes 34 datasets consisting of:

* 1507 Subjects
* 3081 Anatomical Scans
* 4469 Resting Functional Scans
* 1428 Diffusion Scans
* 300 CBF and ASL Scans

For more detailed information about a particular dataset, please see the pages listed below.
