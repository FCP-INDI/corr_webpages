COINS Usage Instructions

To access CoRR data through COINS, go to: http://coins.mrn.org/dataexchange

There, you can login in using your COINS user ID and password (if you do not have an account, just select the "Get Account" option).

From the Data Exchange menu at the top, select Browse Available Data. For users new to COINS, we recommend watching the Video Tutorial available below (also available at the top-left of the Browse Available Data page).

When you are ready, drag an AND Data Group onto the Request Workspace. Into the AND group, drag a Studies data filter. Click Study Name and select CoRR, and click apply. This will ensure you request data from the CoRR samples. It may take up to 6-12 hours for our team to approve your one-time data access request.
