NYU 1 - New York University (Castellanos, Milham)¶

NYU Institute for Pediatric Neuroscience Sample

Sample Description¶

Twenty-five neurotypical adults underwent 3 resting scans as part of a larger study protocol. Subjects also completed IQ testing using the Weschler Abbreviated Scale of Intelligence.
